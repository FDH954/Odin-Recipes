Lasagna Recipe

Lasgagna recipe description here p1

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Meat Sauce
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Boil lasagna noodles in salted water for 15 minutes
  2. Make meat sauce
  3. In oven safe dish, layer noodles with spoonfuls of sauce then cheese
  4. Put in preheated 350F oven for 1 hour
  5. Rest for 15 minutes before enjoying
