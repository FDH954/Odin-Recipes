Pizza Recipe

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Sauce
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Knead dough until you make a disk like shape
  2. Put the Sauce on top of the disk
  3. Put the cheese on top of the sauce, then put the pepperoni and the other toppings on the pizza.
  4. Put the pizza in the ove at 350F for 40 minutes or until golden brown.
  5. Remove Pizza from oven, slice across 4 times to make 8 large slices
