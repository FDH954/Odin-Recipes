Taco Recipe

Taco recipe description here p1

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Beef
  • Packet of Seasonings
  • Cheese
  • Sour Cream
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Put beef onto a pan until brown

  2. Place hard shell tortillas in 350F oven for 10 minutes

  3. After beef is done, put in another bowl to serve
  4. With spoon, put beef into tortillas along with cheese and seasonings as desired
